Frequently asked questions

When should I watch these videos?
Watch these Lectures early on in your NCLEX studies — they give you valuable tips and tricks for the difficult topics.
How many lectures should I watch a day?
We recommend no more than 3 hours of video content of any kind each day. Break up your studies with videos, questions and rationale remediation for the best results.
How do I maximize value while watching the lectures?
Take copious notes! Closed captions are available and you can use the notes section alongside the video player.
Is there an order I should watch these in?
We suggest watching in the order listed. The first video is full of study and prep advice — a great start for anyone.
