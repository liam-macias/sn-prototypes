The Problem

We give students everything.
We guide them nowhere.

1,200 videos. 8,000 questions. Study guides. Readiness assessments. None of it connected. No start, no next step, no signal that you're ready. The current "study plan" is just: take a 75-question test every day.

Today
✓1,200+ videos organized by topic
✓8,000 practice questions
✓Study guides + readiness assessments
✗No connection between any of it
✗No "start here" moment
✗No signal that you're ready
✗Study plan = just take tests every day
✗We never know if students passed their exam
With Learning Loops
→A clear starting point tied to the next exam
→Every session opens with one action to take
→Questions surface weak spots automatically
→Videos assigned by weakness, not browsed
→A qualifying quiz gates exam readiness
→NCLEX plan dynamic, tied to the student's date
→Pass/fail signal we've never had before
→Students stop browsing. They start progressing.
Nursing School Loop

Exam by exam.
Every time.

A student is always studying for something specific. The loop anchors every session to that exam — and only unlocks "ready" when they've actually earned it.

✦AI-Powered Entry (Coming)
Students upload their professor's lecture slides. The AI maps them to relevant SimpleNursing videos and study guides automatically — skipping the self-report step entirely.
What makes this different
Today students browse for content. In the loop, they're assigned it. The qualifying quiz means "exam ready" is a real gate — not a feeling.
Entry
Student picks their exam topic
Self-reported
They're studying for MedSurg Unit 3 — Heart Failure, Cardiac Tamponade, and Hypertension. They select those topics and the system builds a study set.
